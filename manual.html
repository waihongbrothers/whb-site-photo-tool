1. What is this tool? / 这是什么工具？

The WHB Site Photo Tool lets you mark up site inspection photos and generate clean PSTC (Photo Send To Customer) images right from your iPad. No need to wait for the project team to redraw with Adobe Illustrator — you produce the final image directly from the field.

Replaces: the Adobe Illustrator step in the existing Project Team SOP (page 11–21).

这是 PSTC（Photo Send To Customer）的快速制作工具。现场拍照后直接在 iPad 上画线、加颜色、加水印，一键生成图给客户。不用再等 project team 用 Adobe Illustrator 重画。

取代：Project Team SOP（page 11–21）的 Adobe Illustrator 步骤。

2. Quick Start / 快速上手

  1. Project Info — type Customer Name (e.g. JOHN) and last 4 digits of phone (e.g. 0452).
  2. Upload Photos — tap "Choose Files" → select photos from camera roll (any number).
  3. For each photo:
    • Type the Site Address (e.g. 15, JALAN IMPIAN INDAH 9).
    • On the floating dock (top-right of the photo), tap a Tool (e.g. ▭ Rectangle).
    • Tap the Part chip (e.g. ● Water Tank) → pick the part — colour is set automatically.
    • Draw on the photo.
  4. Generate — tap the green Generate Images button at the bottom-right.
  5. Download — single photo = JPG, multiple photos = ZIP. Save to NAS following the existing folder structure.
Tip: The whole process takes about 2 minutes per project once you're used to it.
  1. Project Info — 输入客户名（例 JOHN）和电话末 4 位（例 0452）。
  2. Upload Photos — tap "Choose Files" → 从相册选照片（数量不限）。
  3. 每张照片：
    • 输入 Site Address（例 15, JALAN IMPIAN INDAH 9）。
    • 照片右上角的浮动工具栏（dock）→ 点 Tool（例 ▭ Rectangle）。
    • 点 Part chip（例 ● Water Tank）→ 选 part — 颜色会自动套用。
    • 在照片上画。
  4. Generate — 按右下角绿色 Generate Images 按钮。
  5. 下载 — 单张 = JPG；多张 = ZIP。按现有 NAS 资料夹结构保存。

5. Zoom & Pan / 缩放和平移

  • Pinch with 2 fingers on the photo → zoom in / out, focused on where your fingers are. Use this to zoom into corners (e.g. top-left of the roof).
  • Drag with 2 fingers while pinching → pans the photo. (Same gesture as iOS Photos.)
  • ＋ / − buttons on the dock → zoom in/out (centred on the canvas).
  • 100% button on the dock → reset zoom to default.
Apple Pencil + zoom = the most precise drawing combo. Pinch in to a small detail, then draw a thin line with the Pencil.
  • 双指 pinch 在照片上 → 缩放，焦点在两根手指中间。用来放大角落（屋顶左上角等）。
  • 双指拖动（捏着不放再拖）→ 平移画面。（跟 iOS Photos 一样）
  • Dock 上的 ＋ / − → 以画面中心放大/缩小。
  • Dock 上的 100% → 一键还原。
Apple Pencil + zoom = 最精准的组合。先 pinch 放大细节区域，再用 Pencil 画细线。

6. Watermark, Address & "Private & Confidential" / 水印 / 地址 / 法律文字

Each photo automatically includes:

  • A tiled "Wai Hong Brothers Sdn Bhd" watermark across the photo (in white). Adjust opacity via the Watermark opacity slider below the photo.
  • The Site Address as bold white text with a black outline. Drag it to any corner.
  • The "Private and Confidential" notice. Drag and resize as needed.

To move or resize address / "Private and Confidential":

  1. Tap ✋ Select on the dock.
  2. Tap the text on the photo.
  3. Drag the centre to move; drag corners to resize.

每张照片自动包含：

  • 满屏 "Wai Hong Brothers Sdn Bhd" 水印（白色）。下方有 Watermark opacity slider 调透明度。
  • Site Address：白字黑边粗体。可拖到任何角落。
  • "Private and Confidential" 法律文字。可移动 + 缩放。

要移动/缩放 address 或法律文字：

  1. Dock 切到 ✋ Select。
  2. 点中文字。
  3. 拖中央 = 移动；拖角落 = 缩放。

8. FAQ / 常见问题

Q: I drew the wrong shape. How to remove?

Tap ↶ Undo on the dock to remove the latest shape. Or use ✋ Select → tap the shape → ✕ Delete Selected.

Q: I selected the wrong Part — wrong colour applied.

Tap the Part chip on the dock → pick the right one → continue drawing. Earlier shapes drawn with the wrong colour can be deleted via Select.

Q: My image is blurry after generating.

Make sure you uploaded a high-resolution photo. The tool preserves the original photo's resolution. If your phone camera saved a small file, output will also be small.

Q: Can I save my progress and come back later?

No — the tool doesn't save state. Finish all photos in one session and tap Generate Images before closing the browser tab.

Q: Where do I save the generated images?

Save to NAS following existing project folder structure (refer to Project Team SOP page 60 / Synology Drive).

Q: My iPad is slow when uploading 10+ photos.

Try splitting into smaller batches (e.g. 5 photos at a time). Generate, save, then start a new batch.

Q: Can I print the manual?

Yes — open this manual in Safari → Share → Print. Or save as PDF.

Q：画错了怎么删？

Dock 点 ↶ Undo 撤销最近一笔。或用 ✋ Select → 点中那个图形 → ✕ Delete Selected。

Q：选错 Part 用错颜色了？

点 dock 上的 Part chip → 重选 → 继续画。之前用错色的图形用 Select 删了重画。

Q：生成的 image 模糊？

确保上传的照片清晰。工具会按原图分辨率输出。如果手机拍照是低画质，输出也会模糊。

Q：可以中途存档晚点继续吗？

不行 — 工具不存档。一次性画完所有照片，按 Generate Images 后再关 browser。

Q：生成后保存去哪？

按现有 NAS 资料夹结构存（参考 Project Team SOP page 60 / Synology Drive）。

Q：iPad 上传 10+ 张照片很慢？

分批上传（一次 5 张），生成保存后再开新一批。

Q：手册可以列印吗？

可以 — Safari 开手册 → Share → Print。或存成 PDF。
